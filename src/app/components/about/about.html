About the Teacher

"Bonjour, I am Kanksha Bhuta, a certified C1 French tutor. Being passionate about travel and learning new languages, my journey with the French language began in school and still continues even today as I am a strong believer of the phrase ‘learning never stops’. While pursuing my passion for tourism, what started as a hobby of teaching French, soon turned into a passionate career choice. Having tutored school and college students and having taught the primary section in an international school in Singapore, I have experience of teaching kids as well as adults of different age groups over the last 10 years. I am passionate about teaching and believe that mistakes should be made in order to learn and progress. I provide my students with a safe space to learn and express themselves in the French language. I can speak 5 languages and take the opportunity to learn a new language whenever I can. Apart from teaching, my interests are reading, travelling, music and cooking. "
