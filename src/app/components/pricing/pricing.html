PROGRAMS

Our French Learning Programs

French classes for students, adults, and professionals with a focus on strong foundations, exam preparation, and confident communication.

School Curriculum Support

Aligned with Indian curricula (SSC, HSC, ICSE, CBSE, IB) and UAE curricula (IB, IGCSE, CBSE), helping students build grammar, vocabulary, and exam confidence.

DELF Preparation

Preparation for DELF Prim, Junior, and Adult levels (A1–B2), with structured support in reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills.

Conversation Classes

Interactive French conversation classes for adults to improve fluency, pronunciation, and confidence in everyday communication.

French for Professionals

Specially designed lessons for working professionals who want to use French for career growth, travel, workplace communication, or international opportunities.
